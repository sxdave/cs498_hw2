For Part IV, I measured latency as the toal time a request took from the client to get a response from the server. In order to do this, I created a timestamp before the request was sent and a timestamp after. The steps accounted in the time between included the VM receiving the request, routing whichever request (/register or /list), interactions with Firestore (writing or querying entities), Firestore returning the results and then sending the response back to the client. In order to make this the average time, I repeated both /register and /list 10 times, recorded the spanned time, and then averaged at the end.
